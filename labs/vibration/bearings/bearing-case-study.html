Study Case: Vibration Analysis on Bearings · Fourier & FFT

← Back to Fourier's Lab Portfolio

This study case uses real-world vibration data from bearing failure testing to understand how the Fourier Transform reveals fault signatures such as imbalance, misalignment, looseness, and bearing damage. We will simulate shaft motion, visualize FFT peaks and correlate frequency harmonics with physical vibration modes.
